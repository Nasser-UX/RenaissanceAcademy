Renaissance Academy (RA) aspires to be recognized as a center of learning and service in the education industry. As well, RA works to empower students with the skills and confidence to be contributing members of the business community and society in general. We are keen on efficiently enhancing our resources to improve ourselves, our programs, and our technology.
We are looking forward to inspire and empower teachers to create a new wave of interest towards business, science and technology in young generations. Teachers will be updated with the latest techniques and tools for their class day-to-day activities. School management will be inspired with the added value of such new educational tools.
