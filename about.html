About us

Who We Are

enaissance Academy (RA) aspires to be recognized as a center of learning and service in the education industry. As well, RA works to empower students with the skills and confidence to be contributing members of the business community and society in general. We are keen on efficiently enhancing our resources to improve ourselves, our programs, and our technology.

We are looking forward to inspire and empower teachers to create a new wave of interest towards science and technology in young generations. Teachers will be updated with the latest techniques and tools for their class day-to-day activities. School management will be inspired with the added value of such new educational tools.

Vision

Our vision is wide-ranging. We work with the three key pillars of education industry: the student, the teacher, and school management to reach their full potential, through a nurturing learning environment.

Mission

The mission of Renaissance Academy is to provide high quality educational and professional learning, training and services through distinct courses, educational and professional services, and community interactions that cater for the three pillars of education industry: the student, the teacher, and the school management.

  • Learning - Provide a learning culture that continuously boost all individuals to excel.
  • Relationship - Generate a relationships media among students, families, teachers, school staff and community partners.
  • Integrity - Apply code of conduct based on honesty and responsibility.
  • Accountability - Comply with terms of reference for personal and institutional accountability for student learning, ethical conduct, and adhere to policies and procedures.
  • Innovation - Take distinctive measures in creating unique ideas and innovative solutions in a technology rich environment.
  • Respect - Create a community environment that appreciates the value of students, families, teachers, and school staff.
  • Service - Be client oriented service provider by delivering excellent service.
